About CityFix

CityFix is a citizen-driven platform that empowers people to report everyday city issues—like broken streetlights, open manholes, or garbage pile-ups—to the local authorities. We bridge the gap between residents and city officials for a safer and cleaner urban environment.

Our Mission

To provide an easy-to-use system that enables citizens to raise complaints and receive timely solutions by government authorities.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for civic issue reporting, creating smarter, more responsive, and cleaner cities across the country.

Why CityFix Matters

  • Improves urban safety and hygiene
  • Builds trust between citizens and authorities
  • Promotes transparency and accountability
